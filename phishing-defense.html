The Core Pillars of Phishing Defense

01. Verify the Sender
Always check the actual email address, not just the display name, to ensure it’s from a legitimate source.

02. Inspect Before You Click
Hover over links to see where they really go before clicking. If you aren't expecting the email, don't click at all.

03. Trust Your Gut
If an email feels urgent, uses pressure tactics, or asks for sensitive info, it’s likely a scam. Stop and verify through a trusted channel.
