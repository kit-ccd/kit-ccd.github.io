Our Process: Family Security Framework

01. The Digital Landscape Scan
We identify every device and account in your home to uncover hidden security gaps and shared vulnerabilities.

02. Hardening Your Environment
We configure native parental controls, secure your home router, and set up restricted, age-appropriate user accounts for your children.

03. Family Policy & Culture
We help you draft a Family Media Agreement that establishes tech-free zones and clear, healthy expectations for device use.

04. Monitoring & Maintenance
We provide you with a plan for simple, recurring "Security Syncs" to review permissions and stay ahead of new risks as your children grow.
