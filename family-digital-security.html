Family Digital Security

A professional approach to securing your home, protecting your children's privacy, and building healthy digital habits.

1. The Digital Landscape Scan

We start by identifying every device and account in your home. I’ll help you find those "hidden" security gaps—like shared passwords or outdated software—so we know exactly what we are protecting.

2. Hardening Your Environment

This is the technical work. I configure native parental controls, secure your home router, and set up restricted, age-appropriate user accounts for your children so they have a safe sandbox to explore.

3. Family Policy & Culture

Technology is only half the battle. I help you craft a Family Media Agreement that establishes tech-free zones and sets clear, healthy expectations for how devices are used, ensuring everyone is on the same page.

4. Monitoring & Maintenance

Security is a journey, not a one-time fix. I provide you with a plan for simple, recurring "Security Syncs" to review permissions and stay ahead of new risks as your children grow.
